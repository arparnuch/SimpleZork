Commands are the following:

'info' : output the player and the room he stay information
'quit' : exit from the Game
'help' : display all available commands
'drop <item1>' : drop item name item1
'go <direction>' eg. go east etc.' : go to the direction
'take <item1> <item2> <item3> ....' : player can take multiple items at the same time
'use <potion>' eg. use Green_potion *use with Potion only*
 'attack with <weapon>' eg. attack with Rusty_sword : player attack monster in the room he stay with 'weapon'
